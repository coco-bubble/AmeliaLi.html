ABOUT ME
Lehan Li / Amelia
I am a Media, Culture and Communication student at NYU interested in digital culture, visual storytelling, public relations, and identity in platform-driven spaces. My work explores how media changes the way people experience architecture, perform identity, and imagine themselves online. Across research papers, activism projects, and creative campaigns, I am especially interested in the relationship between aesthetics, visibility, and emotion in the digital age.
Architecture as Media
Research project exploring how social media transforms architecture into image-driven performance and attention economies.
Xiaohongshu & Identity
A cultural analysis of platform aesthetics, algorithmic visibility, and identity formation among young users.
Removing Labels
Interactive activism project challenging gender stereotypes through collective public participation and visual storytelling.
